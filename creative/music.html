Jentry Hagan 2013

This set was done with Miss Jentry Hagan, a local Idahoan vocalist with a beautiful voice. I played guitar, and she did the hard stuff. I expect to record more of her soon.

Live at the Moxie 2012

This set was one of my first live gigs. You can hear the nerves all over the place, but I'll be darned if I didn't have a blast playing it.

What To Do With the Green Man in the Shed 2012

A soundtrack that was done in co-operation with Eli Stonemets. Particularly happy with The Kitchen Scene.

We Will Become Silhouettes 2011

This is an old favorite of mine. Recorded with the internal mic on an Apogee ONE, this puppy came out over a single night. The production is kind of crappy now, but I love it.
